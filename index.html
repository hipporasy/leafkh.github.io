I Am a Mobile Developer

I am Marasy Phi I like to learn to new things, I love solving problems. Throughout my career, I have been driven by my intellectual curiosity to provide solutions to the most pressing problems. I have gone through a lot of different environments, being flexible is hard but fun. Serving only a programming language is like doing the same thing over again and again just to make yourself perfect at it; however, being perfect in one thing doesn’t mean you have to practice that thing again and again. You have to make the perfect practice. In my experience, I wrote Swift for 4 years but I don’t really know how to design POP no matter how many documents I read. Until one day I was developing Java SE, a very old technology, and an open source framework POS (Unicenta). My task was to customize the existing POS from Unicenta, in my mind I thought it was easy task because at school I have learnt Java SE a lot. After reviewed the existing project from Uniceta, I was very shocked because I didn’t understand the project structure, I tried to ask my colleagues and nobody understand it either. It was like I was stuck in the middle of sea. I didn’t give up yet because I was very confident and say I can do it. So, I decided to review the code class by class and understand it bit by bit, after an hour I finally know what to do and I can do it finally. Developing this for fews months then I went back to my own specialist iOS Development, I realized that I didn’t understand about code enough while I was writing Swift back then, I don’t really understand about OOP and I found the answer why I couldn’t improve for year and I have my answer now. I didn’t stop there yet, one of my best friend asked me to develop a front-end website with him because he really need someone to help him. I said okay even though I don’t know how to write it. I and my best friend were discussed about whether we use ReactJs or VueJs. As I had learn a bit about React Native I told him we should go for React JS. It was a hell to me because I didn’t understand how Route worked in React JS. Then I finally be able to finished project on time even though the structure is not good, I realized I had learn a lot... I finally realize how to learn new languages faster, develop new thing faster. My specialist skill is iOS (Swift), and I can develop Android, Web, Hybrid as well. I think I will become FullStack Engineer soon.
